Another obvious form of bias in the list is recency bias, which is probably an artifact of the training corpus comprising mostly internet text. This puts much more weight on things like VueJS that are popular now simply because there are many more training documents mentioning it.

Meanwhile, the list doesn’t even include Edgar Codd, who invented the relational database. Or John Backus, who invented Fortran. These are far more important contributions, but they happened in the 50s and 60s and so they get overlooked.
