Agent Coding lifehack:

Do you have the Logitech MX Master 4 mouse or one of the earlier variants?

(If not, you should consider getting it if you use a computer all day. Logitech probably spends more on R&D for this model of mouse than every other mouse manufacturer on the planet combined, and it shows.)

If you do have the mouse, do you also have a Mac? If so, this trick is for you and saves precious seconds many times per hour, which adds up. It also avoids annoying mistakes like accidentally closing the wrong tab. Speaking of tabs...

If you're running enough agents, then you probably have a lot of tabs. Console tabs in Ghostty/WezTerm for SSH sessions, local terminals, etc. And of course, who doesn't have loads of Chrome tabs open?

There is a second wheel on the MX Master mouse for your thumb. I used to never use it, but it's perfect for switching tabs! In order to set it up on a Mac, you need to buy BetterMouse for a one-time cost of $10 (I have no deal with the creator, I'm just a happy user).

Then you can follow this detailed guide I just posted to set it up nicely to work with the thumbwheel (it even can rotate through your tmux panes, which you can think of as a kind of "virtual tab"):

https://t.co/LLMITZPfZJ
