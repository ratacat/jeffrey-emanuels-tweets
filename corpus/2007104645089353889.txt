My new BrennerBot project started out as a sort of open-ended exploration and celebration of Brenner’s ideas, but has already morphed in just a few days into what is possibly the most advanced and coherent open-source system for harnessing groups of frontier models to work collaboratively on scientific inquiry.

Along the way, I ended up integrating various pieces of my agent flywheel system and tooling, including Agent Mail, ntm for agent orchestration, my cass search tool, and my cass memory (cm) system (all of which you can read about on https://t.co/N4As0kJTQP)
