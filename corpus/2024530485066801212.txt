FrankenTerm is under active development now. It started out as wezterm_automata but has turned into a full-fledge independent terminal emulator based on the best aspects of WezTerm (which it's most directly based on) and Ghostty and Rio.

But every decision is made towards enabling the kinds of crazy high-volume agent swarm workflows that I do without breaking down under load. And it's built on my asupersync library for a rock solid foundation. With plenty of alien artifact characteristics to make it insanely performant.
