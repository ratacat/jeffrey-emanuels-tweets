Two very nice prompts to use with Claude Opus when trying to understand or explain something mathematical:

1) “Now, if you had to distill the absolute essence of how and why <XYZ> works and how it’s able to do <ABC> down to one or two sentences without symbolic math expressions, what would it be?”

2) “If this had never been invented, how might one arrive at it or something very similar starting from first principles?”

See the first two examples below of this prompt applied to Hoeffding’s Dependency, my favorite statistical measure.
